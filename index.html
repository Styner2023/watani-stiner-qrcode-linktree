About Watani Stiner

Watani Stiner, a revolutionary activist, endured 26 years of wrongful imprisonment and exile, sacrificing his freedom for the love of his children and his people. Now, as a writer, poet, and public speaker, he passionately advocates for justice, equality, and freedom.

Through his compelling words and speeches, Watani shares powerful insights on Medium and beyond, inspiring change and raising awareness about critical social issues.
